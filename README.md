# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Objective
Identify top-performing products, regions, and seasonal trends
from the Sample Superstore dataset.

## Key Findings
- West region leads with $739K in sales (32% of total)
- South region underperforms at $391K — potential growth opportunity
- November and December account for 30% of annual revenue
- Canon imageCLASS 2200 is the top single product at $61,600

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)

## Files
- `superstore_analysis.xlsx` — workbook with pivot tables and charts
- `executive_summary.txt` — written findings
